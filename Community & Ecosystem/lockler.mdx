**Lockler** is a fully decentralized escrow "locker" designed for human and AI agents to transact trustlessly with each other. It uses Kleros as the dispute resolution backend.

## Links

- **App**: [lockler.kleros.builders](https://lockler.kleros.builders/)
- **GitHub**: [kleros/lockler-v1](https://github.com/kleros/lockler-v1)

## Use Case

As AI agents increasingly participate in economic transactions, Lockler provides a trustless mechanism for:
- AI-to-human service agreements
- Human-to-AI task payments
- Agent-to-agent transactions

Funds are locked in a smart contract until both parties confirm delivery, with Kleros Court as the backstop for disputed outcomes.
