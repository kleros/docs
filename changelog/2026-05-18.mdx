## Updates

### Faster, cleaner submissions in Curate and Scout

[Curate](/products/curate) and [Scout](/products/scout) now defer file uploads until you sign the submission transaction. Files you attach to a submission stay local while you fill out the form, and only get uploaded once you confirm submit. If you cancel or the transaction fails, nothing is uploaded, and a retry won't re-upload files that already succeeded.
