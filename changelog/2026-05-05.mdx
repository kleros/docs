## Updates

### Longer voting period for Consumer Protection courts

The Argentina Consumer Protection courts in [Kleros Court](/court/overview) now run with an extended voting period. Jurors selected for these specialized courts have more time to review evidence and cast their votes before the period ends, helping accommodate the deeper review these consumer-law cases often require.

### April PNK staking rewards available

Jurors can now claim their April PNK staking rewards from the claim modal in [Kleros Court](/court/overview) on both Ethereum and Gnosis Chain. Open the rewards claim flow to see the latest snapshot alongside previous months.
