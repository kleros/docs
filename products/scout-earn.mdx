# Earn with Kleros Scout

Kleros Scout incentivizes contributors to maintain accurate registries. You can earn rewards both by adding new items to the lists and by verifying assets submitted by other users.

---

## Two Ways to Earn

### 1. Challenges and Bounties

You can earn by identifying problematic submissions. Stake a deposit to challenge an entry that violates the registry rules; if you win the dispute through Kleros Court, you earn a portion of the submitter's deposit as a bounty.

<Warning>
Incorrect challenges result in losing your own deposit. Always verify the registry policy before challenging.
</Warning>

### 2. Reward Programs

Kleros and partner projects run periodic campaigns to reward contributors for adding high-quality entries. Monthly incentive updates (pools, eligible chains, and parameters) are published on the blog. Monitor these channels for active campaigns:

- The [Kleros Scout blog posts](https://blog.kleros.io/tag/kleros-scout/) - monthly incentive program updates
- The [Kleros Scout interface](https://app.klerosscout.eth.limo/)
