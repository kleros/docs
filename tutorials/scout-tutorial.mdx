# Scout Tutorial

This tutorial is a complete walkthrough of using [Kleros Scout](https://app.klerosscout.eth.limo/): submitting new entries, challenging invalid ones, managing challenged submissions, and removing fraudulent entries.

---

## Submitting a New Entry

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the platform">
    Visit the Kleros Scout dApp and select your registry: Tokens, Address Tags, or Contract-Domain Names.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the policy requirements">
    Read the registry-specific rules via the **Registry Details** button. Gather the necessary documentation, links, and proof materials before you start.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the submission form">
    Click **Submit entry** and fill out all required fields accurately, uploading your collected information and evidence.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pay the deposit and confirm">
    Deposit xDai (or the applicable token) and complete the wallet transaction.

    <Warning>
    Always double-check facts before submitting. If your submission is incorrect, you lose your deposit.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monitor the challenge period">
    Your entry enters **Registration Requested** status. If no one challenges it during the set timeframe, it is approved into the registry.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Challenging a Submitted Entry

<Steps>
  <Step title="Identify suspicious entries">
    Browse the registry for entries in **Registration Requested** status that look invalid.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Collect evidence">
    Gather links, screenshots, and documents proving the entry violates the registry policy.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Challenge the entry">
    Open the entry via the **Details** button and press **Challenge Entry**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the challenge form">
    Provide strong evidence for the Kleros Court review, and confirm with sufficient funds in your wallet.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The entry moves to **Challenged** status and is resolved through Kleros Court. If you win, you earn a portion of the submitter's deposit; if you lose, you forfeit your own.

## Managing Challenged Submissions

Whether you are the submitter or the challenger:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Stay updated">
    Track the submission status once it is marked **Challenged**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit additional evidence">
    Select the entry and click **Submit Evidence** to strengthen your case.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monitor deadlines">
    Watch evidence updates and dispute deadlines. After the deadline, jurors decide.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Appeal if needed">
    If you believe the decision was incorrect, appeal options are available.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Removing Confirmed Fraudulent Entries

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate the entry">
    Navigate to the relevant registry and open the questionable entry via **Details**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Cross-reference the policy">
    Verify that the entry actually violates the registry's policy requirements.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Request removal">
    Press **Remove Entry**, complete the form with your evidence, and make sure you have adequate funds for the deposit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Complete the wallet transaction. The entry enters **Removal Request** status, and the outcome is determined by the community (and Kleros Court if challenged).
  </Step>
</Steps>
